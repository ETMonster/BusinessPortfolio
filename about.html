About Me

I am a passionate believer that computer programming can solve any problem it faces. Coding isn’t only mindless ones and zeros, it’s endless sequences of logic and math stringed together to create something amazing that everyone around the world uses every day, all day.
After programming for over five years, I can say that I’ve learned many valuable skills like critical thinking and problem-solving along the way. I have always wanted to be more capable whether it's learning a new programming language, app, or technique.
I am an aviation enthusiast learning at the yoke and throttle and always striving to create graphic designs using programs like Photoshop and Blender. Succeeding my two-year Scratch coding career, I learned web development, which is what created this website for the BTT10 course at Bell High School. After dabbling in game development, I finished one game with 14 downloads on Itch.io.
An avid thirteen-year-old developer, I am devoted to coding, aviation, and creating art for the world to see.
