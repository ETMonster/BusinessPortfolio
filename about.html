About Me

I am a passionate believer that computer programming can overcome any problem it faces. Coding isn’t only mindless ones and zeros. It’s endless sequences of logic and math strung together to create something amazing that everyone around the world uses all day, every day.
After programming for over five years, I can say I’ve learned many valuable skills like critical thinking and problem-solving along the way. I have constantly wanted to be more capable whether it's grasping a new programming language, app, or technique.
I am an aviation enthusiast learning at the yoke that is always striving to produce graphic designs using programs like Photoshop and Blender. Succeeding my two-year Scratch coding career, I learned web development, which is what created this website for the BTT10 course at Bell High School. After dabbling in video game development, I completed one game with 14 downloads on Itch.io.
An avid thirteen-year-old developer, I am devoted to coding, aviation, and creating art for the world to see.
